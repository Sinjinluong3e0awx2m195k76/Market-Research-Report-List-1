There are two main types of EGR coolers used in the automotive industry: tube EGR coolers and finned EGR coolers. Tube EGR coolers consist of tubes that carry exhaust gases through a cooling chamber, while finned EGR coolers have fins that increase surface area for better heat dissipation.
