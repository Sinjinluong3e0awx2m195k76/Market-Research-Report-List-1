<p><p>PC/ASA resin is a polymer alloy blend of polycarbonate (PC) and acrylonitrile styrene acrylate (ASA) resins. It offers a combination of properties from both resins, including high impact resistance, excellent weatherability, and good heat resistance. PC/ASA resin is widely used in applications such as automotive exterior components, electrical housings, and construction materials.</p><p>The global PC/ASA resin market is experiencing steady growth, driven by the increasing demand for durable and weather-resistant materials in various industries. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.9% during the forecast period. The automotive sector is a major consumer of PC/ASA resin, particularly for exterior components such as bumpers, grilles, and trim parts. The construction industry is also a prominent market for PC/ASA resin due to its excellent weatherability and UV resistance.</p><p>Some of the latest trends in the PC/ASA resin market include the development of innovative grades with enhanced properties, such as improved heat resistance and impact strength.
