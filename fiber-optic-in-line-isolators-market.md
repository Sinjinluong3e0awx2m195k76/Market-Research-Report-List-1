<p><ul><li>Single Stage Isolator</li><li>Two Stage Isolator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber Optic In-Line Isolators are devices used to prevent the back reflection of light in fiber optic systems. Single Stage Isolators have one isolator stage, while Two Stage Isolators have two isolator stages. Single Stage Isolators are simpler and more cost-effective, suitable for applications where minimal isolation is required. Two Stage Isolators offer higher isolation levels and are ideal for demanding environments where stronger protection against back reflection is needed. Both types serve the purpose of maintaining signal integrity and optimizing the performance of fiber optic systems.</p></p>
