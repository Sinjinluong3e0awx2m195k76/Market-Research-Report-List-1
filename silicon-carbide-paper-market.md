<p><p>Silicon Carbide Paper comes in two main types - Self-adhesive and Non-adhesive. Self-adhesive Silicon Carbide Paper is coated with an adhesive backing that allows for easy application and removal on surfaces, making it convenient for users. On the other hand, Non-adhesive Silicon Carbide Paper requires the use of additional adhesives or clamps to secure it to surfaces. Non-adhesive paper offers more versatility in terms of applications and can be easily customized to fit different needs. Both types of Silicon Carbide Paper have their advantages and are used in various industries for sanding and polishing purposes.</p></p>
