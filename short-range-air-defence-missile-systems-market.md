<p><p>Short-Range Air-Defence Missile Systems market includes two main types: Man-Portable Air-Defense Systems (MANPADS) and Stationary/Platform-Mounted Air-Defense Systems. MANPADS are lightweight and portable missile systems operated by individuals or small teams. They can be shoulder-launched and are designed to engage low-flying aircraft or helicopters. On the other hand, Stationary/Platform-Mounted Air-Defense Systems are fixed or mounted on various platforms like vehicles, ships, or buildings. These systems provide air-defense capabilities to military bases, critical infrastructures, or mobile military units.</p></p>
