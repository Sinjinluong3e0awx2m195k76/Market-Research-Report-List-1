<p><p>Automotive windscreen glazing market consists of laminated glass, tempered glass, and polycarbonate types. Laminated glass is constructed with a layer of plastic sandwiched between two layers of glass, providing enhanced safety as it prevents shattering upon impact. Tempered glass undergoes a special heating and cooling process which strengthens it, making it resistant to breaking. Polycarbonate, a durable and lightweight material, is increasingly being used in automotive windscreen glazing due to its impact resistance and flexibility, making it suitable for various applications.</p></p>
