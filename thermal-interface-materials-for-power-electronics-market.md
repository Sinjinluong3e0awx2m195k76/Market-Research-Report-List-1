<p><p>Thermal Interface Materials for Power Electronics are used to improve heat transfer between electronic components and heat sinks. There are two main types in the market: Silicone-based and Non-silicone. Silicone-based materials offer high thermal conductivity and are commonly used for power electronics applications.
