<p><strong>The Fibre Optic Cables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-mode Optical Cable</li><li>Multimode Optical Cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fibre optic cables market includes two main types: single-mode optical cable and multimode optical cable. Single-mode cables are designed for long-distance transmission with a narrow core size, providing high bandwidth and low attenuation. Multimode cables have a larger core size, allowing multiple modes of light to travel through at the same time, making them suitable for shorter distances and lower bandwidth needs. Both types play a crucial role in providing efficient and reliable data transmission in various industries.</p></p>
