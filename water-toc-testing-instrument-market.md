<p><p>Water TOC testing instruments are categorized into various types based on the method used for measuring Total Organic Carbon (TOC) in water samples. These types include NDIR method, UV method, Conductance method, and others. NDIR method utilizes Infrared spectroscopy to measure organic carbon content, UV method uses Ultraviolet light absorption, Conductance method measures total organic carbon through conductivity, while other methods may include combustion or persulfate oxidation. Each method offers unique benefits and applications in water quality monitoring and analysis.</p></p>
