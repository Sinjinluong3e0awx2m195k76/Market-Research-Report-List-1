<p><p>Standard Recovery Rectifiers are electronic components that convert alternating current (AC) to direct current (DC). They are classified into two main types: Axial Standard Recovery Rectifiers and Surface Mount Device (SMD) Standard Recovery Rectifiers. Axial Standard Recovery Rectifiers are traditional through-hole components, while SMD Standard Recovery Rectifiers are smaller and more suitable for modern compact electronic devices. Both types are widely used in various applications such as power supplies, inverters, and battery chargers.</p></p>
