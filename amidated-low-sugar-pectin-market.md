<p><strong>The Amidated Low Sugar Pectin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Methoxyl (HM) Pectin</li><li>Low Methoxyl (LM) Pectin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Amidated Low Sugar Pectin is available in two main market types: High Methoxyl (HM) Pectin and Low Methoxyl (LM) Pectin. HM Pectin is commonly used in high sugar preserves and jams due to its ability to form gels in the presence of sugar. On the other hand, LM Pectin is used in low sugar and sugar-free products, as it can create gels in the presence of calcium ions instead of sugar. Both types of pectin offer different functionalities and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the product being developed.</p></p>
