<p><strong>The Magnesium Wheel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cast Magnesium Wheel</li><li>Forged Magnesium Wheel</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The magnesium wheel market can be categorized into three types: cast magnesium wheel, forged magnesium wheel, and others. Cast magnesium wheels are made through pouring molten magnesium into a mold, resulting in a cost-effective option for manufacturers. Forged magnesium wheels, on the other hand, are produced through a process of shaping and compressing solid magnesium, resulting in a lighter and stronger wheel option. The "others" category includes any magnesium wheel types that do not fit into the cast or forged categories.</p></p>
