<p><ul><li>Natural Gas or Liquid Propane</li><li>Air-Cooled vs. Liquid Cooled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Standby generators are available in two main types based on fuel source: natural gas or liquid propane. Natural gas generators use a constant supply from a utility line, while liquid propane generators store fuel on site. Additionally, generators can be categorized as air-cooled or liquid-cooled based on their cooling methods. Air-cooled generators are typically smaller, more compact, and simpler to maintain, while liquid-cooled generators are larger, more powerful, and more efficient for extended use.</p></p>
