<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The RFID Digital Lock Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Contactless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RFID digital locks are classified into contact and contactless markets based on how they operate. Contact RFID locks require physical contact between the lock and the RFID key for authentication, while contactless RFID locks use radio frequency signals for communication, allowing the key to be simply waved in front of the lock for access. Both types offer secure and convenient methods of access control, with contactless locks being more user-friendly and faster to use compared to contact locks.</p></p>
