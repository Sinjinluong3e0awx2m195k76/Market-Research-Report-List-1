<p><p>PPS filaments are categorized into two main market types: multifilament and monofilament. Multifilament PPS filaments are made up of multiple fine strands twisted together, offering increased strength and flexibility. They are commonly used in applications requiring durability and resistance to high temperatures. On the other hand, monofilament PPS filaments consist of a single thicker strand, providing better control over stiffness and consistency. They are often used in precise applications where uniformity and smoothness are crucial.</p></p>
