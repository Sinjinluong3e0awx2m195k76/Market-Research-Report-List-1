<p><p>Permanent bonding materials market consists of three main types: die-attach adhesives, thermally conductive adhesives, and structural bonding adhesives. Die-attach adhesives are used for bonding semiconductor components to substrates. Thermally conductive adhesives aid in heat dissipation by transferring heat from components to heat sinks. Structural bonding adhesives are used for joining materials like metals, plastics, and composites. Each type serves a specific purpose in bonding applications, providing strong and lasting bonds for various industries like electronics, automotive, and construction.</p></p>
