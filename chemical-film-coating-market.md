<p><p>Chemical film coatings are broadly categorized into primer and topcoat markets. Primers are applied initially to prepare the substrate surface for further coating, promoting adhesion and corrosion resistance. Topcoats are applied on top of primers to provide additional functionalities such as color, gloss, and protection against environmental factors. Both primer and topcoat coatings play a crucial role in enhancing the durability and aesthetics of various products, making them essential components in industries like automotive, aerospace, and construction.</p></p>
