<p><p>Auto glass is a specialized type of glass that is used for the windows, windshield, and other glass components of vehicles. It is designed to provide clarity and visibility while ensuring the safety of passengers. Auto glass is typically made of laminated safety glass, which consists of two layers of glass with a layer of polyvinyl butyral (PVB) sandwiched in between. This design helps prevent the glass from shattering upon impact.</p><p>The Auto Glass Market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing production and sales of vehicles globally.
