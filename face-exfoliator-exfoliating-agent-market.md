<p><p>Face exfoliator exfoliating agents are classified into two main types: physical exfoliating agents and chemical exfoliating agents. Physical exfoliating agents consist of abrasive particles or tools that physically scrub away dead skin cells. On the other hand, chemical exfoliating agents use acids or enzymes to dissolve dead skin cells. Both types of exfoliating agents serve the same purpose of removing dead skin cells and promoting cell turnover, but they work through different mechanisms. Consumers can choose between these two types based on their skin type and preferences.</p></p>
