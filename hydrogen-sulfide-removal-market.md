<p><p>Hydrogen sulfide removal market includes two main types: regenerative and non-regenerative. In regenerative systems, the media used to remove hydrogen sulfide can be easily regenerated for reuse, making them more cost-effective in the long run. On the other hand, non-regenerative systems require the media to be replaced after use, resulting in higher operating costs. Both types of systems are commonly used in various industries such as oil and gas, wastewater treatment, and chemical processing for removing hydrogen sulfide from gas streams.</p></p>
