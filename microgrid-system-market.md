<p><ul><li>AC Microgrid System</li><li>DC Microgrid System</li><li>Hybrid Microgrid System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>AC Microgrid Systems utilize alternating current for energy distribution, typically integrating with the main utility grid. DC Microgrid Systems operate on direct current for local energy distribution, often used in off-grid communities or remote areas. Hybrid Microgrid Systems combine both AC and DC components for increased flexibility in managing energy generation and consumption. These systems are gaining popularity due to their ability to optimize energy efficiency, reliability, and resilience, making them a key player in the transition towards decentralized and sustainable energy systems.</p></p>
