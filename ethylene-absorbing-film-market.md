<p><p>Ethylene-absorbing film market types include Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene (BOPP), Polypropylene (PP), Polyester (PET), and Polyethylene (PE). Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene (BOPP) films are known for their high tensile strength and durability, making them suitable for packaging applications. Polypropylene (PP) films offer good barrier properties and moisture resistance. Polyester (PET) films are thermally stable and have high clarity, making them ideal for food packaging. Polyethylene (PE) films are flexible and have good sealability, making them versatile for various packaging needs.</p></p>
