<p><ul><li>Direct Contact Type</li><li>non-contact Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Speed sensing switches are devices that detect the speed of an object or machinery and trigger a response accordingly. These switches come in two main types: direct contact and non-contact. Direct contact switches physically come into contact with the moving object to determine its speed, while non-contact switches use sensors such as infrared or magnetic to detect speed without direct contact. Both types of switches are widely used in various industries for monitoring and controlling the speed of equipment and machinery.</p></p>
