<p><p>Paintball equipment market consists of various types including markers/guns, masks/goggles, hoppers/loaders, paintballs, barrels, and others. Markers/guns are the primary equipment used to shoot paintballs, while masks/goggles protect players' eyes and face. Hoppers/loaders store and feed paintballs into the markers/guns. Paintballs are spherical capsules filled with paint used as ammunition. Barrels are attachments to the markers/guns that affect accuracy and distance. Other equipment may include protective gear, clothing, and accessories for players.</p></p>
