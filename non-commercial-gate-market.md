<p><p>Non-commercial gate market types include driveway gates and crash (wedge barrier) gates. Driveway gates are typically used to secure residential properties and provide a decorative entrance to a driveway. Crash gates, also known as wedge barriers, are heavy-duty gates designed to prevent unauthorized vehicle access by physically blocking the path of oncoming vehicles. Both types of gates serve different purposes in terms of security and access control, with driveway gates focusing on aesthetics and privacy, while crash gates prioritize high-level security and vehicle control.</p></p>
