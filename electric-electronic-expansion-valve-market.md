<p><ul><li>Direct Acting</li><li>Decelerating Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric Electronic Expansion Valve Market Types include Direct Acting and Decelerating Type. Direct Acting valves operate directly based on the input signals they receive, allowing for quick and accurate adjustments. On the other hand, Decelerating Type valves operate by gradually adjusting the valve opening in response to input signals, resulting in a slower but more controlled response. Both types are widely used in HVAC and refrigeration systems, providing efficient and precise control over the flow of refrigerant in the system.</p></p>
