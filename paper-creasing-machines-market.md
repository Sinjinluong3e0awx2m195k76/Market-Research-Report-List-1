<p><p>Paper creasing machines are available in two main types: manual and electric. Manual creasing machines require the user to manually operate the machine by pulling a lever or pressing down on a handle to create the crease. Electric creasing machines, on the other hand, are powered by electricity and automatically create the crease at the push of a button. Both types of machines are widely used in printing shops, binderies, and offices for creasing paper before folding to ensure neat and professional-looking documents.</p></p>
