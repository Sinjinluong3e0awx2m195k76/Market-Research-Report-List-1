<p><p>Dicing Die Attach Film market types include Non-Conductive Type and Conductive Type. Non-Conductive Type films are designed to provide insulation between the die and substrate, preventing electrical conduction. On the other hand, Conductive Type films facilitate electrical conduction between the die and substrate. These two types cater to different requirements in the semiconductor industry, with Non-Conductive Type films being ideal for applications where electrical isolation is crucial, while Conductive Type films are used when electrical conduction is necessary.</p></p>
