<p><p>Laminating adhesives are used in various industries for bonding layers of materials together. The market can be categorized into three types: solvent-based laminating adhesives, solventless laminating adhesives, and water-based laminating adhesives. Solvent-based adhesives use organic solvents to create a strong bond, while solventless adhesives do not require solvents for curing. Water-based adhesives are environmentally friendly and have low volatile organic compound emissions. Each type of adhesive offers different benefits and is used in different applications based on their properties.</p></p>
