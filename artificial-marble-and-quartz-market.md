<p><p>Artificial marble and quartz market types can be categorized into cement artificial marble and resin type artificial marble. Cement artificial marble is produced by mixing marble powder, cement, and color pigments, resulting in a durable and cost-effective material. On the other hand, resin type artificial marble is manufactured using a combination of resin, calcium powder, and natural pigments, which offers a high level of customization and design flexibility. Both types of artificial marble are widely used in various applications such as countertops, flooring, and wall cladding.</p></p>
