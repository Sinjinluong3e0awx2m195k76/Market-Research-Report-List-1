<p><p>Orthopaedic shoes cater to various foot conditions, with different types serving specific needs. Flatfoot orthopaedic shoes provide support for fallen arches, while Cavus shoes are designed for high arches. Calcaneal spur shoes help alleviate heel pain, Varus shoes correct inward foot alignment, and Valgus shoes address outward foot alignment. Additionally, there are other types of orthopaedic shoes available in the market that cater to different foot issues such as plantar fasciitis, bunions, and arthritis, offering comfort and support for those with specialized foot conditions.</p></p>
