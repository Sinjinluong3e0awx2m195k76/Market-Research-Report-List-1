<p><ul><li>Unilateral</li><li>Binaural</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bionic ears are available in two market types - Unilateral and Binaural. Unilateral bionic ears are designed for individuals who have hearing loss in only one ear, providing amplification and sound enhancement for the affected ear. Binaural bionic ears, on the other hand, are designed for individuals with hearing loss in both ears, offering synchronized and balanced sound processing for improved hearing capabilities. Both types of bionic ears aim to enhance the hearing experience and improve overall quality of life for those with hearing impairments.</p></p>
