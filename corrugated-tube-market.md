<p><p>Corrugated tubes are widely categorized into two main types: Single Wall Corrugated (SWC) and Double Wall Corrugated (DWC). SWC tubes consist of a single layer of corrugated paper, providing basic protection and cushioning. On the other hand, DWC tubes have an additional layer of corrugated paper, offering enhanced strength and durability. Both types are commonly used in packaging and transportation industries to protect goods from damage during handling and shipping.</p></p>
