<p><p>Variable-frequency drives (VFDs) are categorized based on the voltage level they operate at. Low-voltage VFDs are used in applications where the power supply is up to 690 volts. Medium-voltage VFDs are designed for power supplies ranging from 1 to 15 kilovolts, while high-voltage VFDs are used for power supplies exceeding 15 kilovolts.
