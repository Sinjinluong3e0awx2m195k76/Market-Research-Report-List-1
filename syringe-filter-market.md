<p><ul><li>Pore Size: 0.22 μm</li><li>Pore Size: 0.45 μm</li><li>Pore Size: 0.8 μm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Syringe filters are used in various industries for filtration purposes. The market types based on pore size include 0.22 μm, 0.45 μm, and 0.8 μm. These numbers represent the size of the pores in the filter membrane. A pore size of 0.22 μm is used for sterilization and removing bacteria and microorganisms. A pore size of 0.45 μm is suitable for removing particles such as particles, precipitates, and inorganic ions. Finally, a pore size of 0.8 μm is used for filtration of larger particles and for clarifying solutions.</p></p>
