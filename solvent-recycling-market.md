<p><strong>The Solvent Recycling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>On-Site Solvent Recycling</li><li>Off-Site Solvent Recycling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solvent recycling market is categorized into two types: On-Site Solvent Recycling and Off-Site Solvent Recycling. On-site solvent recycling involves the installation of solvent recovery equipment at the customer's facility to regenerate used solvents for reuse within the same premises. Off-site solvent recycling involves collecting used solvents from the customer's facility, transporting them to a recycling facility for processing, and then returning the regenerated solvents back to the customer. Both types aim to help companies reduce waste disposal costs and minimize environmental impact.</p></p>
