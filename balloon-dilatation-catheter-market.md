<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Balloon dilatation catheter market is divided into two types: Type I and Type II. Type I market includes high-pressure balloons used for dilatation of coronary arteries. These balloons are designed for higher-pressure inflation and have improved maneuverability. Type II market encompasses low-pressure balloons used in dilatation of peripheral arteries and veins. These balloons are designed for lower-pressure inflation and allow for increased flexibility in navigating narrow or complex vascular structures. Both types of balloons play crucial roles in various cardiovascular procedures.</p></p>
