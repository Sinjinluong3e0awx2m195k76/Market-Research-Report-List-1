<p><strong>The Optical Proximity Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Digital Output Optical Proximity Sensors</li><li>Analog Output Optical Proximity Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical proximity sensors are available in two main types: digital output and analog output. Digital output sensors provide a simple on/off signal based on the presence or absence of an object within a certain distance. These sensors are commonly used in applications where precise distance measurement is not critical. Analog output sensors, on the other hand, provide a continuous output signal that varies based on the distance between the sensor and the object. These sensors are used in applications where accurate distance measurement is required.</p></p>
