<p><strong>The Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) Earphone Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Feedforward Active Noise Canceling</li><li>Feedback ANC</li><li>Hybrid ANC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) Earphone market consists of three main types: Feedforward ANC, Feedback ANC, and Hybrid ANC. Feedforward ANC uses an external microphone to pick up ambient noise and generates anti-noise to cancel it out. Feedback ANC employs an internal microphone to pick up sound leakage from the earphone and cancels it. Hybrid ANC combines both feedforward and feedback methods to deliver superior noise-canceling performance. Each type offers unique features and benefits to cater to different user preferences and environments.</p></p>
