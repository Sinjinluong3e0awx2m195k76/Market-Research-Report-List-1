<p><strong>The Programmable Delay Lines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SMD</li><li>Through Hole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Programmable Delay Lines are available in two main market types: SMD (surface mount device) and Through Hole. SMD Delay Lines are designed to be mounted directly onto the surface of a printed circuit board, allowing for efficient and space-saving installation. On the other hand, Through Hole Delay Lines have leads that are inserted through holes in the circuit board and then soldered in place, providing a more secure and durable connection. Both types of Delay Lines cater to different needs and preferences within the electronics industry.</p></p>
