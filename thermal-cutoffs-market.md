<p><strong>The Thermal Cutoffs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Fuses</li><li>Thermal Switches</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal cutoffs are safety devices that are used to protect electrical appliances from overheating. There are three main types of thermal cutoffs on the market: thermal fuses, thermal switches, and other types of thermal cutoff devices. </p><p>Thermal fuses are one-time use components that are designed to permanently cut off the flow of electricity when a certain temperature is reached. Thermal switches, on the other hand, are reusable devices that can be reset once the temperature has returned to normal. Other types of thermal cutoffs include polymers, ceramics, and bimetals, each with their own specific applications and advantages in protecting electronic devices from overheating.</p></p>
