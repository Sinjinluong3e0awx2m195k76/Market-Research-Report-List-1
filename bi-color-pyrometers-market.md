<p><p>Bi-color pyrometers are devices used for measuring high temperatures in industrial processes. Optical bi-color pyrometers operate by comparing thermal radiation at two different wavelengths to determine the temperature of an object. Infrared bi-color pyrometers use infrared radiation to measure temperature without contact. Both types of pyrometers are commonly used in industries such as manufacturing, metalworking, and ceramics production. They are relied upon for their accuracy, efficiency, and non-contact measurement capabilities in monitoring and controlling high-temperature processes.</p></p>
