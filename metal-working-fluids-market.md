Metal Forming Fluids: These fluids facilitate the shaping of metal through processes like rolling, extrusion, and stamping.</p><p>4. Metal Protecting Fluids: These fluids are used to prevent corrosion and extend the lifespan of metal equipment and components.</p></p>
