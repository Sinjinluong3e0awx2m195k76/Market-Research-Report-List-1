<p><ul><li>Confocal Sensor Controller</li><li>Confocal Sensor Head</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Point chromatic confocal sensors are divided into two main market types: the confocal sensor controller and the confocal sensor head. The confocal sensor controller is responsible for managing and controlling the functions of the sensor, such as receiving and processing data from the sensor head. On the other hand, the confocal sensor head is the physical component that actually measures the distance to the target surface using the confocal technology. Both components work together to provide highly accurate and precise measurements in various industrial applications.</p></p>
