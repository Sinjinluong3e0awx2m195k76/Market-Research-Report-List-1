<p><p>High Energy Cyclotron market can be categorized into two types - Single Particle Cyclotron and Multi Particle Cyclotron. Single Particle Cyclotrons are designed to accelerate a single particle, typically a proton or a deuteron, to high energy levels for various applications like medical imaging and cancer treatment. On the other hand, Multi Particle Cyclotrons are designed to accelerate multiple particles simultaneously, allowing for a wider range of research and industrial applications such as isotope production and material irradiation.</p></p>
