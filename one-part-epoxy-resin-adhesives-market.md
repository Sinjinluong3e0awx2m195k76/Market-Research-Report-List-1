<p><ul><li>Heat Curing</li><li>Ultraviolet Curing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>One-part epoxy resin adhesives are commonly categorized based on their curing methods, including heat curing and ultraviolet (UV) curing. Heat curing adhesives require elevated temperatures to initiate the curing process, making them ideal for applications where heat can be easily applied. On the other hand, UV curing adhesives rely on exposure to ultraviolet light for curing, enabling fast and efficient bonding without the need for external heat sources. Both types offer unique benefits in terms of handling, speed, and performance for various bonding applications.</p></p>
