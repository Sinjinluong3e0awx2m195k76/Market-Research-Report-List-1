<p><p>ZigBee modules are classified into three main types based on their frequency bands: 868MHz, 900MHz, and 2.4GHz. The 868MHz ZigBee modules offer good range and penetration capabilities, making them suitable for industrial and smart metering applications. The 900MHz ZigBee modules are commonly used in home and building automation due to their good balance of range and data rate. The 2.4GHz ZigBee modules provide high data rates and are ideal for consumer electronics and smart home devices. Each market type caters to specific applications and requirements.</p></p>
