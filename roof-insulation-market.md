<p><p>The roof insulation market can be divided into four main types: Glass Wool, Rock Wool, Foam, and Other materials. Glass Wool insulation is made from recycled glass and is known for its thermal and acoustic insulation properties. Rock Wool insulation is made from volcanic rock and steel slag, providing fire-resistant and moisture-repelling benefits. Foam insulation is typically made from polyurethane or polystyrene, offering high thermal resistance and moisture protection. Other materials in the market include natural fibers and reflective insulation.</p></p>
