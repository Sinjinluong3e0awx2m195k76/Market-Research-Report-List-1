<p><p>Automotive cylinder liners and cylinder sleeves are crucial components in the engine block that protect the cylinder walls from wear and tear. Cylinder liners are typically made from cast iron and provide a smooth surface for the piston to move up and down. On the other hand, cylinder sleeves are thin-walled metal tubes that are inserted into the engine block to restore the cylinder bore to its original size. Both of these products play a vital role in maintaining the performance and longevity of the engine.</p></p>
