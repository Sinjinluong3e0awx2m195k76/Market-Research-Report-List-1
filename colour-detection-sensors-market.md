<p><p>Colour detection sensors are used in various industries for different applications. Brightness sensors are used to detect the intensity of light, while contrast sensors are used to distinguish between different shades of colors. Molecular luminescence sensors detect the presence of specific molecules based on their luminescent properties. RGB sensors use Red, Green, and Blue light to detect colors accurately. Other types of color detection sensors include infrared sensors, ultraviolet sensors, and multispectral sensors, each serving different purposes in color detection applications.</p></p>
