<p><p>The Face Shield market can be categorized into two main types: Full Face Shield and Half Face Shield. Full Face Shields provide complete coverage from the forehead to the chin, offering maximum protection against splashes, droplets, and other hazards. On the other hand, Half Face Shields cover only the upper half of the face, leaving the lower half exposed. Each type caters to different user preferences and requirements, with Full Face Shields being commonly used in healthcare settings and Half Face Shields being used in industries where visibility and breathability are important.</p></p>
