<p><p>Silicon-based photodetectors are widely used in various applications due to their high sensitivity and low noise performance. Two key types in the market are Silicon Drift Detector (SDD) and Silicon Photomultiplier (SiPM). SDDs are commonly used in X-ray spectroscopy and electron microscopy for high-resolution imaging and material analysis. SiPMs are preferred in medical imaging and scintillation detection due to their excellent photon counting capabilities and fast response time. Both types offer unique advantages in their respective applications, driving their demand in the market.</p></p>
