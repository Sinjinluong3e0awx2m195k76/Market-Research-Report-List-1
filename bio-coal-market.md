<p><strong>The Bio-coal Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid Cylindrical Type</li><li>RUF Type</li><li>Pini-Kay Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bio-coal market types include solid cylindrical type, RUF type, and Pini-Kay type. Solid cylindrical bio-coal is compacted into cylindrical shapes for easy handling and transportation. RUF bio-coal is formed into denser blocks, ensuring efficient combustion and longer burning time. Pini-Kay bio-coal is characterized by its unique shape, with a hole in the center for improved airflow. Each type offers different benefits in terms of efficiency, ease of use, and environmental impact, catering to varying consumer preferences in the bio-coal market.</p></p>
