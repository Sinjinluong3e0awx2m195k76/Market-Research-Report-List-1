<p><strong>The Controlled Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pneumatic Controlled Valves</li><li>Electric Controlled Valves</li><li>Hydraulic Controlled Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Controlled valves market consists of three main types: Pneumatic Controlled Valves, Electric Controlled Valves, and Hydraulic Controlled Valves. Pneumatic controlled valves use air pressure to regulate flow, suitable for applications requiring quick response times. Electric controlled valves are powered by electricity, offering precise control over flow and direction. Hydraulic controlled valves use fluid pressure for operation, ideal for high-pressure applications. Each type caters to different industries and applications, providing customers with a range of options to choose from based on their specific needs.</p></p>
