<p><strong>The MDI Prepolymers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MDI ( Polyether)</li><li>MDI ( Polyester)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MDI Prepolymers are primarily categorized into two types: MDI (Polyether) and MDI (Polyester). MDI (Polyether) prepolymers are based on polyether polyols and are known for their superior flexibility and low temperature flexibility. On the other hand, MDI (Polyester) prepolymers are based on polyester polyols and are valued for their high resistance to chemicals and heat. Both types of MDI prepolymers are widely used in various industries such as automotive, construction, and furniture for their unique properties and applications.</p></p>
