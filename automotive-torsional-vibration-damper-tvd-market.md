<p><p>Automotive Torsional Vibration Damper (TVD) market includes clutch type, DMF type, and others. Clutch type TVDs are designed to dampen torsional vibrations between the engine and transmission, while DMF type TVDs contain a dual mass flywheel for enhanced vibration absorption. Other types of TVDs may include viscous dampers or elastomer dampers. Each type of TVD serves to reduce harmful vibrations, improve overall vehicle performance, and increase the lifespan of drivetrain components.</p></p>
