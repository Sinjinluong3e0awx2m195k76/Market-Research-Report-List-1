<p><p>Wax paper is divided into two main market types: dry wax paper and wet wax paper. Dry wax paper is commonly used for packaging dry foods such as sandwiches or baked goods, as it has a wax coating on only one side. On the other hand, wet wax paper is used for packaging wet or greasy foods, as it is coated with wax on both sides to prevent liquids from seeping through. Both types of wax paper offer moisture resistance and are commonly used in foodservice and food packaging industries.</p></p>
