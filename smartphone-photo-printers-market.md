<p><strong>The Smartphone Photo Printers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Dye Sublimation Printer</li><li>Inkjet Printer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smartphone photo printers are available in two main types - Thermal Dye Sublimation Printer and Inkjet Printer market. Thermal Dye Sublimation Printers use a heating element to transfer dye onto paper, resulting in high-quality prints with vibrant colors. They are faster and more efficient, making them ideal for printing photos on the go. In contrast, Inkjet Printers use liquid ink sprayed onto paper to create prints. They are more budget-friendly but can have slower printing speeds and may not offer the same level of color vibrancy as thermal dye sublimation printers.</p></p>
