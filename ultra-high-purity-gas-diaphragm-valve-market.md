<p><p>Ultra high purity gas diaphragm valves are classified into two main types based on pressure requirements: high pressure and low pressure valves. High pressure valves are designed to handle gases at elevated pressures, typically above 1000 psi, ensuring reliable performance and durability in demanding industrial applications. On the other hand, low pressure valves are suitable for gases operating at pressures below 1000 psi, providing precise control and leak-tight sealing for applications that do not require high-pressure handling. Both types cater to different pressure needs in various industries.</p></p>
