<p><p>Time Temperature Indicator (TTI) Labels are used in various industries to track the temperature history of a product. Color-based TTIs consist of color-changing indicators that show if a specific temperature threshold has been exceeded. Barcode-based TTIs use barcode technology to provide detailed information about temperature changes over time. Both types of TTIs are designed to help ensure the quality and safety of products by indicating if they have been exposed to temperature conditions that may compromise their integrity.</p></p>
