<p><ul><li>Unidirectional Stretching</li><li>Biaxial Stretching</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Microporous film market types include unidirectional stretching and biaxial stretching. In unidirectional stretching, the film is only stretched in one direction, resulting in superior tensile strength and elongation properties along that axis. Biaxial stretching involves stretching the film in both machine and transverse directions, creating a balanced and uniform film with enhanced barrier properties and higher overall performance. Both types of stretching play a crucial role in determining the physical characteristics and application suitability of microporous films in various industries.</p></p>
