<p><p>The offshore wind power installation vessel market consists of three types: self-propelled jack-up vessels, normal jack-up vessels, and heavy lift vessels. Self-propelled jack-up vessels have their own propulsion system, enabling them to move without assistance. Normal jack-up vessels require tugs or pusher boats for transportation. Heavy lift vessels, on the other hand, are mainly used for transporting and installing heavy components of wind turbines. Each vessel type plays a critical role in the installation and maintenance of offshore wind farms.</p></p>
