<p><p>Automotive Logic ICs are available in two main market types based on their mounting styles: SMD (Surface Mount Device) and SMT (Surface Mount Technology). SMD mounting style refers to components that are attached to the surface of a printed circuit board, providing a compact and efficient design. SMT mounting style, on the other hand, involves the use of advanced technologies to mount components directly onto the board, improving reliability and performance. Both market types offer a range of options for automotive applications.</p></p>
