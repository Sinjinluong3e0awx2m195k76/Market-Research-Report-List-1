<p><p>Tie-Rod Hydraulic Cylinder market is segmented into three main types: Single Acting, Double Acting, and Multi-Stage/Tandem. Single Acting cylinders apply force in one direction, while Double Acting cylinders apply force in both directions. Multi-Stage/Tandem cylinders have multiple stages to achieve higher force output. Single Acting cylinders are commonly used in applications requiring simple linear motion, while Double Acting cylinders are suitable for applications needing force in both directions. Multi-Stage/Tandem cylinders are used when higher force output is required.</p></p>
