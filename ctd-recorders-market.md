<p><strong>The CTD Recorders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Profiling CTD</li><li>Moored CTD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CTD recorders are used for measuring conductivity, temperature, and depth in the ocean. Profiling CTD recorders are used for conducting real-time measurements of these parameters at different depths while the instrument is lowered through the water column. Moored CTD recorders are fixed at a specific depth for extended periods of time to continuously monitor the water column. Both types of recorders are essential in oceanographic research to understand changes in physical properties of the ocean.</p></p>
