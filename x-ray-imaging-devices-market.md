<p><strong>The X-ray Imaging Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DR</li><li>CR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>X-ray imaging devices market consists of two main types: Digital Radiography (DR) and Computed Radiography (CR). DR systems use electronic detectors to capture and produce high-quality images quickly, without the need for film development. CR systems use storage phosphor plates to capture images, which are then scanned and processed digitally. Both DR and CR systems offer benefits such as improved image quality, faster image results, and reduced radiation exposure compared to traditional film-based X-ray imaging.</p></p>
