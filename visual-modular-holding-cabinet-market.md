<p><ul><li>Bottom Heat</li><li>Top and Bottom Heat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Visual Modular Holding Cabinets are available in different market types based on the heating mechanism used. The Bottom Heat market type involves heating the cabinet from the bottom only, ensuring that food items are evenly warmed and maintained at the desired temperature. On the other hand, the Top and Bottom Heat market type includes heating elements located both at the top and bottom of the cabinet, providing more consistent heating and temperature control for a wider variety of foods. Both types offer efficient and reliable ways to keep food hot and fresh until serving.</p></p>
