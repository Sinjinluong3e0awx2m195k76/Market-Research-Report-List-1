<p><p>Balloon catheters are used in various medical procedures to open blocked or narrowed blood vessels. Types of balloon catheters include Normal Balloon Catheter, which is a standard catheter used for angioplasty procedures, Cutting Balloon Catheter, which has small blades to help break up plaque in the artery, Scoring Balloon Catheter, which creates tiny cuts in the plaque to help open up the vessel, Drug Eluting Balloon Catheter, which releases medication to prevent re-narrowing of the vessel, and Stent Graft Balloon Catheter, which is used to deploy stent grafts in the treatment of aneurysms.</p></p>
