<p><strong>The Polyurethane Resin Composite Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pultrusion</li><li>Winding</li><li>Perfusion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyurethane Resin Composite market includes three main types: Pultrusion, Winding, and Perfusion. Pultrusion involves continuous production of composite materials with a uniform cross-section. Winding refers to the process of wrapping fibers around a mandrel to create cylindrical composite structures. Perfusion involves injecting resin into a mold containing reinforcement fibers to create composite parts. Each of these processes offers unique advantages in terms of production efficiency, material properties, and applications within the polyurethane resin composite market.</p></p>
