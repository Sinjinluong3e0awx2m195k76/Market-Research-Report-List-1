<p><p>Foam life jackets, commonly referred to as personal flotation devices (PFDs), come in various types to cater to different preferences and needs. The two main types in the market are vest type foam life jackets and yoke type foam life jackets. Vest type foam life jackets are designed like a vest, providing full coverage and freedom of movement. Yoke type foam life jackets have a yoke-shaped design that distributes the buoyancy around the neck and shoulders, offering added comfort and support in the water.</p></p>
