<p><strong>The Self-contained Breathing Apparatus Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open-Circuit SCBA</li><li>Closed-Circuit SCBA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Self-contained Breathing Apparatus (SCBA) market consists of two main types: Open-Circuit SCBA and Closed-Circuit SCBA. </p><p>Open-Circuit SCBA, also known as demand valve systems, are commonly used by firefighters and emergency responders. It involves users breathing in air from an attached cylinder and exhaling it out into the atmosphere. </p><p>Closed-Circuit SCBA, on the other hand, is primarily used in military applications and confined spaces. It operates by recycling exhaled air, removing carbon dioxide and replenishing it with oxygen, allowing for longer durations of use.</p></p>
