Light grade open gear lubricants are typically used for low-load applications and offer high speed lubrication. Medium grade open gear lubricants are suitable for moderate load applications, while heavy grade open gear lubricants are designed for high load and slow speed applications. Each type is formulated to provide optimal protection and lubrication for different types of open gears in various industrial settings.</p></p>
