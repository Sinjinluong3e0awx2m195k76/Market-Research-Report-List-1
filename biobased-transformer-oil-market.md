<p><p>The biobased transformer oil market consists of two main types: natural ester transformer fluid and synthetic ester transformer oil. Natural ester transformer fluid is derived from renewable sources such as vegetable oils, offering environmental benefits and improved fire safety compared to traditional mineral oils. Synthetic ester transformer oil, on the other hand, is chemically synthesized to provide enhanced performance characteristics such as better oxidation stability and compatibility with various materials. Both types offer sustainable alternatives to conventional transformer oils, catering to the growing demand for eco-friendly solutions in the power industry.</p></p>
