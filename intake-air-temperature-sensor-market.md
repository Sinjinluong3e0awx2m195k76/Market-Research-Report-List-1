<p><p>Intake-Air Temperature Sensor Market includes three types:</p><p>1. Integrated Into Manifold Absolute Pressure Sensor: This type combines both intake-air temperature and manifold pressure sensing into a single sensor, reducing the need for multiple sensors in the engine system.</p><p>2. Standalone Intake-Air Temperature Sensor: This type is a separate sensor solely dedicated to measuring the intake-air temperature, providing more precise data compared to integrated sensors.</p><p>3. Integrated Into Mass Air Flow Sensor: This type combines intake-air temperature sensing with mass air flow measurement, offering a comprehensive data collection solution for engine performance analysis.</p></p>
