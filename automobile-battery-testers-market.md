<p><p>The automobile battery testers market consists of three types based on voltage: 6V, 12V, and 24V. A 6V battery tester is specifically designed to diagnose and test 6-volt batteries commonly found in vintage or smaller vehicles. A 12V battery tester is the most common type and is suitable for diagnosing and testing the 12-volt batteries used in most modern cars. A 24V battery tester is designed for heavy-duty vehicles, such as trucks and buses, which are equipped with a 24-volt battery system instead of the standard 12-volt system.</p></p>
