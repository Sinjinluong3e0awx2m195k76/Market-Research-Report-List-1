Long glass fiber reinforced polypropylene is known for its high strength and impact resistance, making it suitable for applications requiring superior performance. On the other hand, short glass fiber reinforced polypropylene offers improved dimensional stability and surface finish. Both types of glass-reinforced polypropylene provide excellent strength-to-weight ratios and are commonly used in automotive, industrial, and consumer goods industries.</p></p>
