<p><strong>The Hard-Coated Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Longpass Filter</li><li>Shortpass Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hard-coated filters are widely used in various industries for their durability and efficiency in blocking unwanted wavelengths of light. Longpass filters allow longer wavelengths to pass through while blocking shorter wavelengths, making them ideal for applications like fluorescence microscopy. Shortpass filters, on the other hand, allow shorter wavelengths to pass through while blocking longer wavelengths, making them suitable for applications like machine vision systems. Both types of filters play a crucial role in enhancing image clarity and improving overall performance in different optical systems.</p></p>
