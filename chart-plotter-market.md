<p><strong>The Chart Plotter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>GPS Based Chart Plotter</li><li>GNSS Based Chart Plotter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>GPS based chart plotters utilize information from the Global Positioning System to accurately track a vessel's location and display it on a map. On the other hand, GNSS based chart plotters use data from multiple Global Navigation Satellite Systems, such as GPS, GLONASS, Galileo, and BeiDou, to provide more precise positioning information. Both types of chart plotters are essential tools for navigation and are widely used in marine environments for safe and efficient sailing.</p></p>
