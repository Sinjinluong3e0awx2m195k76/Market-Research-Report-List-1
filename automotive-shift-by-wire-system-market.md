<p><p>Automotive shift-by-wire system market consists of various types of components including steering wheel assembly, turn to executive assembly, and main controller (ECU). Steering wheel assembly is responsible for manual gear shifting and transmission control. Turn to executive assembly enables the driver to electronically select the driving mode. The main controller (ECU) serves as the central processing unit that coordinates the functioning of all the shift-by-wire system components. These components work together to provide a seamless and efficient driving experience.</p></p>
