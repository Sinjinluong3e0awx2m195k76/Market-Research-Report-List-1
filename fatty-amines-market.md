<p><p>Fatty amines are categorized into three main types: primary, secondary, and tertiary fatty amines. Primary fatty amines are derived from fatty acids and have one alkyl chain attached to the nitrogen atom. Secondary fatty amines have two alkyl chains attached to the nitrogen atom, while tertiary fatty amines have three alkyl chains attached. Each type of fatty amine has different applications and properties, making them popular in various industries such as personal care, agriculture, and water treatment.</p></p>
