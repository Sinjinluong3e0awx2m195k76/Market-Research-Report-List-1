<p><p>Self-adhesive heat shields come in various types to meet specific needs in different automotive applications. Turbo heat shields are designed to protect components from high temperatures generated by turbochargers. Underbody heat shields provide insulation from exhaust heat for the underside of vehicles. Header heat shields protect engine components from heat radiated by exhaust manifolds. Exhaust pipe heat shields are used to prevent heat damage to surrounding parts. Each type serves a unique purpose in protecting vehicle components from heat damage.</p></p>
