<p><p>Micro centrifuge tubes are widely used in laboratories for centrifugation and storage of small volume samples. There are three main types of market bases: conical base, round bottom base, and flat bottom base. Conical base tubes are designed for efficient pelleting of precipitates, while round bottom tubes are commonly used for mixing and stirring applications. Flat bottom tubes are ideal for stability on benches and thermal cycler blocks. Each type serves specific purposes and caters to different laboratory needs.</p></p>
