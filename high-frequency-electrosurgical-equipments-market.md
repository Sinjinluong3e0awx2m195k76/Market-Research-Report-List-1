<p><ul><li>Monopolar Circuit</li><li>Bipolar Circuit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High Frequency Electrosurgical Equipments Market is segmented into Monopolar Circuit and Bipolar Circuit based on the types of circuits used in the equipment. Monopolar Circuit is used for cutting and coagulating tissues with one active electrode and a dispersive electrode outside the surgical site. Bipolar Circuit is used for precise and controlled coagulation during surgery with both electrodes placed close to each other within the surgical site. Both types of circuits play important roles in electrosurgical procedures, catering to different surgical needs.</p></p>
