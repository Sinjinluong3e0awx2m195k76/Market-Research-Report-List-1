<p><p>Traditional lubricating oils are derived from crude oil and are refined to meet specific viscosity and performance requirements. Hydrogenated lubricants are processed with hydrogen gas to improve stability and oxidation resistance. Isomerization dewaxing lubricants undergo a process to rearrange the molecular structure for better low-temperature properties. Each type of lubricating oil has its own advantages and applications, catering to a wide range of industries and vehicles. The diversity in lubricating oil types allows for customized solutions to meet the specific needs of various machinery and equipment.</p></p>
