<p><p>The hands-free power liftgate market can be divided into two types: kicking and non-kicking. In the kicking market, the liftgate is triggered to open when the user kicks a designated area under the rear bumper, using motion detection technology. This allows for a touchless and convenient experience, especially when the user's hands are occupied. On the other hand, the non-kicking market involves alternative methods, such as sensors that detect the user's presence or a button that needs to be pressed for the liftgate to open. Both types provide users with the flexibility of accessing the liftgate without physically touching it.</p></p>
