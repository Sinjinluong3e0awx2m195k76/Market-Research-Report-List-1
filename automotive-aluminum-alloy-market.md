<p><p>The automotive aluminum alloy market can be categorized into three main types: cast aluminum alloy, rolled aluminum alloy, and extruded aluminum alloy. Cast aluminum alloy is formed by pouring molten aluminum into a mold, rolled aluminum alloy is produced by passing aluminum ingots through rollers to create desired shapes, and extruded aluminum alloy is formed by forcing aluminum through a die to create specific profiles. Each type of aluminum alloy offers unique properties and is used in different automotive applications.</p></p>
