<p><p>Bi-directional amplifiers are devices used to enhance wireless communication in places with poor signal coverage. There are two main types of bi-directional amplifiers: full-duplex and half-duplex. </p><p>Full-duplex bi-directional amplifiers allow for simultaneous transmission and reception of signals, making them more efficient for two-way communication. </p><p>On the other hand, half-duplex bi-directional amplifiers can only transmit or receive signals at one time, making them less efficient for real-time communication. </p><p>Each type has its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific communication needs of the user.</p></p>
