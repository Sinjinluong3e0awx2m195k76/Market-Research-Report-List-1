<p><strong>The Automatic Continuous Variable Transmission Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Belt Connection</li><li>Chain Connection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic Continuous Variable Transmission (CVT) systems have two main types in the market: Belt Connection and Chain Connection. Belt Connection CVTs use a belt to smoothly change gear ratios without discrete steps, providing seamless acceleration and improved fuel efficiency. On the other hand, Chain Connection CVTs use a metal chain to transmit power and change gear ratios continuously. Both types offer advantages in terms of performance, fuel efficiency, and smooth driving experience, catering to different preferences of consumers in the automotive industry.</p></p>
