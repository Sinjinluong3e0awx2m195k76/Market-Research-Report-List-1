<p><p>Boat lacquer market consists of two main types: 1-component boat lacquer and 2-component boat lacquer. 1-component boat lacquer is a single-part product that is ready to use and requires no mixing, making it more convenient and easier to apply. On the other hand, 2-component boat lacquer consists of two parts that need to be mixed together before application, providing a more durable and longer-lasting finish. Both types offer excellent protection against harsh marine conditions, but 2-component boat lacquer generally provides a higher level of performance and durability.</p></p>
