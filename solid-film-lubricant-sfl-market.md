<p><p>Solid Film Lubricants (SFL) come in three main types: PTFE, Molybdenum Disulfide, and Graphite. PTFE type lubricants are known for their low friction and high resistance to chemical reactions. Molybdenum Disulfide type lubricants offer excellent resistance to wear and extreme pressure conditions. Graphite type lubricants are known for their ability to withstand high temperatures and provide smooth lubrication. Each type of SFL caters to specific lubrication needs, making them preferred choices in various industries.</p></p>
