<p><strong>The Disabled Crutches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Forearm Crutch</li><li>Underarm Or Axilla Crutch</li><li>Platform Crutch</li><li>Leg Support Crutch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The disabled crutches market can be categorized into four main types. The Forearm Crutch, also known as an elbow crutch, provides support through the forearm and is commonly used for long-term disabilities. The Underarm or Axilla Crutch rests under the armpit and is suitable for short-term injuries. Platform Crutches have a larger base for added stability and are commonly used by people with balance issues. Leg support crutches provide additional support for individuals with lower extremity injuries. Each type caters to specific needs and preferences within the disabled community.</p></p>
