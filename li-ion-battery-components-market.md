<p><p>The Li-ion battery components market consists of four main types: cathode, anode, electrolyte, and separator. The cathode is the positive electrode that stores lithium ions during charging. The anode is the negative electrode that releases lithium ions during discharging. The electrolyte is the liquid or solid medium that allows the flow of ions between the cathode and anode. The separator is the physical barrier that keeps the cathode and anode from touching and causing a short circuit.</p></p>
