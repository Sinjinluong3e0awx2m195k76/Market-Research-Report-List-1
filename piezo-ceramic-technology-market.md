<p><p>Piezo ceramic technology market types include Lead Zinc Titanates (PZT), Lead Titanate (PT), Lead Magnesium Niobate (PMN), and others. PZT is the most commonly used material due to its high piezoelectric properties. PT is known for its high electromechanical coupling factor. PMN offers high sensitivity and low hysteresis. Other materials in the market include Barium Titanate and Lead Zirconate Titanate. Each type of piezo ceramic material has unique characteristics and applications, catering to a wide range of industries such as automotive, medical, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
