<p><p>The CAD CAM milling machine market consists of different types based on the number of axes they possess: 5-axis, 4-axis, and 3-axis. In a 5-axis milling machine, the cutting tool can move in five directions, enabling complex and precise milling. 4-axis machines allow movement in four directions, while 3-axis machines move in three directions only, limiting their milling capabilities. These different types cater to various industries and applications, offering varying levels of flexibility, efficiency, and accuracy.</p></p>
