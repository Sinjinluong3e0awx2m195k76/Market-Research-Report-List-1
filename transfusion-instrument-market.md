<p><p>The transfusion instrument market includes various types of infusion pumps, such as volumetric infusion pumps and gravity-delivered infusion pumps. Volumetric infusion pumps accurately control the rate and volume of fluid delivery to patients, providing precise dosages of medication or fluids. Gravity-delivered infusion pumps rely on gravity to control the flow rate, typically used for simpler infusions. Both types of pumps play a critical role in delivering fluids, medications, and blood products to patients in healthcare settings.</p></p>
