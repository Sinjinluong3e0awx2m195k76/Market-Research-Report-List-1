<p><ul><li>Plenum Vaporizers</li><li>Drawover Vaporizers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Anesthetic vaporizers are devices used in medical settings to deliver volatile anesthetics to patients. There are two main types of anesthetic vaporizers: plenum vaporizers and drawover vaporizers. Plenum vaporizers have a separate chamber where the anesthetic is vaporized before being delivered to the patient, while drawover vaporizers rely on the patient's breathing to draw the anesthetic through the device. Both types of vaporizers are essential tools in anesthesia administration, providing precise control over the delivery of anesthetic gases.</p></p>
