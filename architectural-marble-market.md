<p><p>Architectural marble market consists of two main types: natural marble and artificial marble. Natural marble is derived from quarried stone and is highly sought after for its elegance and unique veining patterns. It is known for its durability and timeless beauty. On the other hand, artificial marble is manufactured using a combination of marble dust and resins. It is more affordable and comes in a wide range of colors and patterns. Both types of marble cater to different preferences and budgets in the architectural industry.</p></p>
