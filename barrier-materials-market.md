<p><p>Barrier materials are crucial for preserving the quality and extending the shelf life of food and beverage products. The market for barrier materials can be categorized into different types, including PVDC, EVOH, PEN, and others. PVDC is known for its excellent barrier properties against moisture and gases. EVOH offers high oxygen barrier properties. PEN is known for its excellent heat resistance and transparency. Other barrier materials include aluminum foil and metallized films, which provide a combination of barrier properties. Each type of barrier material has its unique advantages and applications in the packaging industry.</p></p>
