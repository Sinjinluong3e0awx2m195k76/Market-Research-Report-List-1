<p><p>Vehicle detectors are devices used to detect the presence of vehicles on roads or in parking lots. Different types of vehicle detectors include inductive loop detectors, which use an electromagnetic field to sense vehicles; ultrasonic detectors, which use sound waves to detect vehicles; magnetic detectors, which detect changes in the Earth's magnetic field caused by vehicles; radar detectors, which use radio waves to detect vehicles; and other types such as video detectors. Each type of detector has its own advantages and applications in traffic management and parking systems.</p></p>
