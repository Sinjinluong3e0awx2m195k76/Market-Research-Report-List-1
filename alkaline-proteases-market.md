<p><p>Alkaline proteases are enzymes that break down proteins in an alkaline environment. There are two main types of alkaline proteases on the market: serine proteases and metalloproteases. Serine proteases contain a serine amino acid in their active site, while metalloproteases require a metal ion for their activity. Both types of proteases are used in various industries such as food processing, pharmaceuticals, and detergents for their ability to break down proteins effectively in alkaline conditions.</p></p>
