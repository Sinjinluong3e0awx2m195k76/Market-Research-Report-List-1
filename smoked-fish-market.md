This type of smoked fish is great for immediate consumption and can be eaten hot or cold. Cold smoked fish, on the other hand, is cured with salt and then smoked at lower temperatures, resulting in a delicate texture and subtle smoky flavor. This type of smoked fish is typically served cold and is perfect for appetizers or as part of a charcuterie board. Both hot and cold smoked fish are popular in the market for their unique flavors and versatility.</p></p>
