<p><p>Chemical indicator tape is used to monitor the sterilization process in healthcare facilities. There are different types of chemical indicator tapes available for specific sterilization methods, such as steam sterilization and ethylene oxide (EO) sterilization. Steam sterilization tapes change color when exposed to high temperatures and steam, indicating that sterilization has occurred. EO sterilization tapes change color when exposed to ethylene oxide gas, confirming the sterilization process. Both types of indicator tapes provide visual confirmation that sterilization has been successful.</p></p>
