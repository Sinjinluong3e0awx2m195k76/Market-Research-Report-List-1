<p><p>Video pyrometers are devices used to measure the temperature of an object without making physical contact. There are two main types of video pyrometers on the market: fixed and handheld. Fixed video pyrometers are installed in a specific location and continuously measure the temperature of objects passing by. Handheld video pyrometers are portable devices that can be moved around for temperature measurement at various locations. Both types of video pyrometers are used in industrial settings for tasks such as monitoring furnace temperatures and ensuring product quality.</p></p>
