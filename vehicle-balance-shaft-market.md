<p><ul><li>Forged</li><li>Cast Balance Shaft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vehicle balance shafts play a critical role in reducing vibrations and ensuring smoother engine operation. The two main types of balance shafts used in vehicles are Forged and Cast balance shafts. Forged balance shafts are manufactured by shaping heated metal using high pressure, resulting in a stronger and more durable product. On the other hand, cast balance shafts are produced by pouring molten metal into a mold and allowing it to cool and solidify, offering a cost-effective alternative. Both types are essential components in maintaining the balance and stability of engines.</p></p>
