<p><p>Premixed grout comes in two main types: epoxy and non-epoxy. Epoxy premixed grout is known for its durability, resistance to stains, and ability to adhere well to various surfaces. It is commonly used in high-traffic areas and for projects requiring a high level of strength. Non-epoxy premixed grout, on the other hand, is easier to work with, dries faster, and is more affordable. It is suitable for most standard tiling projects and is available in a wide range of colors to match different tiles and aesthetics.</p></p>
