<p><strong>The Injection Molding Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Injection Molding Machines</li><li>Hydraulic Injection Molding Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Injection molding machines market can be categorized into two main types: mechanical injection molding machines and hydraulic injection molding machines. Mechanical injection molding machines are driven by cams, gears, or levers, providing precise control over the molding process. On the other hand, hydraulic injection molding machines use hydraulic power to operate the machine, offering high pressure, fast cycling times, and improved efficiency. Both types of machines have their own advantages and are widely used in the manufacturing industry.</p></p>
