There are three main types of GaN FETs in the market - Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor Field-Effect Transistor (MOSFET), Heterojunction Field Effect Transistor (HFET), and Modulation Doped Field Effect Transistor. MOSFETs are known for their simple structure and low power consumption, HFETs offer high electron mobility and fast switching speeds, while Modulation Doped FETs provide high electron density and improved performance in high-frequency applications.</p></p>
