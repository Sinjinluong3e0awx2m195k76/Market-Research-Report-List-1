<p><p>Survival lighters come in various types such as electric lighters, fuel lighters, and plasma lighters. Electric lighters are rechargeable and provide a flameless and windproof ignition. Fuel lighters use traditional fuel sources like butane or lighter fluid for ignition. Plasma lighters use an electric current to produce a high-intensity heat source for fire starting. Each type of survival lighter offers unique advantages and is suitable for different outdoor situations, making them essential tools for camping, hiking, and other survival situations.</p></p>
