<p><p>Special mortar market can be divided into two main types: Dry Special Mortar and Wet Mixed Special Mortar. Dry Special Mortar is pre-mixed dry powder that only requires the addition of water on site before use. This type offers longer shelf life, easy storage, and convenient transportation. On the other hand, Wet Mixed Special Mortar is pre-mixed with water and ready to use upon delivery. It offers faster application and more consistent quality control. Both types cater to different construction needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
