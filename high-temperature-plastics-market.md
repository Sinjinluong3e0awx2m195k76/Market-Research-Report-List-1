<p><p>High temperature plastics market includes various types such as fluoropolymers, polyphenylene sulfide, polysulfone, polyimides, and others. Fluoropolymers are known for their excellent thermal stability and chemical resistance. Polyphenylene sulfide offers high mechanical strength and dimensional stability at elevated temperatures. Polysulfone is valued for its high impact strength and resistance to chemicals. Polyimides are popular for their exceptional heat resistance and electrical insulation properties. Other high temperature plastics include materials like polyether ether ketone (PEEK) and polyetherimide (PEI) which offer unique characteristics suitable for high temperature applications.</p></p>
