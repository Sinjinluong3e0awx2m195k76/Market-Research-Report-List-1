<p><p>Magnet rings are commonly classified into two main market types: Radial and Axial. Radial magnet rings have magnetic poles located on the outer surface in a radial arrangement, providing a strong magnetic field in all directions. This design is ideal for applications requiring uniform magnetic flux distribution. On the other hand, Axial magnet rings have magnetic poles arranged along the axial direction, making them suitable for applications requiring a strong magnetic field along the axis of rotation. Both types have their own unique advantages and are used in various industries based on specific requirements.</p></p>
