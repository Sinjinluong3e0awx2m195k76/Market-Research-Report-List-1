<p><p>Cementitious adhesive in the ceramic tile market is known for its strong bonding properties and is commonly used for interior and exterior tiling applications. Dispersion adhesive offers flexibility and is suitable for large format tiles. Reaction resin adhesive is ideal for areas with high moisture levels and provides excellent resistance to chemicals. Each type of adhesive caters to different tiling needs, making it crucial for consumers to choose the right one based on the specific requirements of their project.</p></p>
