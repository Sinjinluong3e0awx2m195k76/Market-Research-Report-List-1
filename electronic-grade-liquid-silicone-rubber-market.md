<p><strong>The Electronic Grade Liquid Silicone Rubber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Component LSR</li><li>Two Component LSR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electronic Grade Liquid Silicone Rubber is available in two main types - Single Component LSR and Two Component LSR. Single Component LSR is a ready-to-use material, requiring no mixing, making it convenient and cost-effective. On the other hand, Two Component LSR involves pre-mixing two separate components to initiate the curing process, providing more control over the curing time and temperature. Both types offer high purity, excellent electrical properties, and thermal stability, making them ideal for electronic applications.</p></p>
