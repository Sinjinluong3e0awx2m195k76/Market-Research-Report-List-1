<p><ul><li>Non-Biological</li><li>Biological</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pod detergent comes in two main types: Non-Biological and Biological. Non-Biological pods do not contain enzymes and are generally recommended for people with sensitive skin or allergies. They work by using a combination of surfactants and other cleaning agents to remove stains and dirt. On the other hand, Biological pods contain enzymes that break down proteins and other organic materials, making them more effective at removing tough stains like grass or blood. Both types are popular choices for convenient and efficient laundry cleaning.</p></p>
