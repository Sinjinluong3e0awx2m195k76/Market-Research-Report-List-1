<p><p>Hall-Effect Current Sensor market consists of two main types: Linear (Analogous) Electroflu Detectors and Threshold (Digital) Electroflu Detectors. Linear Electroflu Detectors provide a continuous output signal proportional to the current being measured, allowing for precise measurements. On the other hand, Threshold Electroflu Detectors provide a digital output signal when the current exceeds a predetermined threshold, offering a simple on/off indication of current presence. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the market for current sensing technology.</p></p>
