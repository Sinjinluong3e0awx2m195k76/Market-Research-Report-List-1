<p><p>Carbon Fiber Medical Composites market is segmented into Thermosetting Type and Thermoplastic Type. Thermosetting composites involve polymers that undergo a chemical reaction, becoming rigid and stable once cured. They are commonly used in medical devices due to their high strength and stiffness. On the other hand, Thermoplastic composites are able to be repeatedly melted and reshaped without losing their strength, making them ideal for applications that require flexibility and ease of processing in the medical field.</p></p>
