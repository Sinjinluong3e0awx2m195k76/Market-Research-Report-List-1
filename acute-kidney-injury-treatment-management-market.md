<p><strong>The Acute Kidney Injury Treatment & Management Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Prerenal Acute Kidney Injury</li><li>Intrinsic Renal Acute Kidney Injury</li><li>Postrenal Acute Kidney Injury</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Acute Kidney Injury (AKI) can be classified into three types based on the underlying cause: Prerenal AKI, Intrinsic Renal AKI, and Postrenal AKI. Prerenal AKI occurs due to decreased blood flow to the kidneys, often caused by dehydration or heart failure. Intrinsic Renal AKI is the result of direct damage to the kidney tissues, commonly caused by conditions like sepsis or certain medications. Postrenal AKI is due to obstruction of urine flow from the kidneys, often caused by conditions like kidney stones or tumors. Treatment and management of AKI depend on identifying and addressing the underlying cause.</p></p>
