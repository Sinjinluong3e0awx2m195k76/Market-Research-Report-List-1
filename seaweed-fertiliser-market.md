<p><p>Seaweed fertiliser market involves various types of extraction methods such as chemical hydrolysis, physics extraction method, enzymolysis process, and biological fermentation process. Chemical hydrolysis involves the use of chemicals to break down seaweed biomass. Physics extraction method uses physical processes like drying and grinding. Enzymolysis process involves the use of enzymes to extract nutrients from seaweed. Biological fermentation process utilizes microorganisms to break down seaweed. Each method has its own advantages and applications in the seaweed fertiliser market.</p></p>
