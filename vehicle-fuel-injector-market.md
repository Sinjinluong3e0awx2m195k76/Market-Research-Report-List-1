<p><ul><li>Gasoline Direct Injection (GDI)</li><li>Port Fuel Injectors (PFI)</li><li>Diesel Fuel Injectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vehicle Fuel Injector Market includes three main types: Gasoline Direct Injection (GDI), Port Fuel Injectors (PFI), and Diesel Fuel Injectors. GDI injects fuel directly into the combustion chamber for better fuel efficiency and power. PFI injects fuel into the intake port before the intake valves, providing good performance and reliability. Diesel Fuel Injectors deliver fuel into the combustion chamber under high pressure, maximizing efficiency and power output. Each type of fuel injector caters to different fuel types and engine requirements.</p></p>
