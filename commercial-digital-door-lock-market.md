Magnetic Stripe Locks use magnetic cards for access control, Electromechanical Door Locks use electric signals to control access, and Electric Strike Locks operate by releasing a locking mechanism when an electric current is applied. These types of locks provide advanced security features and convenience for commercial spaces, making them popular choices in the market.</p></p>
