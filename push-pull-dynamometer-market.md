<p><p>The push-pull dynamometer market consists of two main types: electronic medical dynamometers and mechanical medical dynamometers. Electronic medical dynamometers utilize electronic sensors to measure and record force exerted during muscle testing, providing precise data for medical professionals. On the other hand, mechanical medical dynamometers rely on mechanical components such as springs and gauges to measure force. Both types of dynamometers are widely used in physical therapy, sports medicine, and rehabilitation settings to assess muscle strength and monitor progress during treatment.</p></p>
