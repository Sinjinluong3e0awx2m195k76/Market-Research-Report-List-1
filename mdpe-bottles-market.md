<p><p>MDPE bottles come in two main types: narrow mouth and wide mouth. Narrow mouth MDPE bottles are typically used for storing liquids or powders that need to be dispensed in controlled amounts, such as pharmaceuticals or chemicals. Wide mouth MDPE bottles have a larger opening, making them suitable for products that need to be accessed easily, like food ingredients or cleaning supplies. Both types of bottles are commonly used in industries such as pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, and chemicals.</p></p>
