<p><ul><li>Reverse Osmosis Membranes (RO)</li><li>Ultrafiltration Membranes (UF)</li><li>Microfiltration Membranes (MF)</li><li>Nano-Filtration Membranes (NF)</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Membrane filtration market types include Reverse Osmosis Membranes (RO), which are used to remove dissolved salts and other impurities; Ultrafiltration Membranes (UF) that separate larger molecules from water; Microfiltration Membranes (MF) which remove suspended solids; and Nano-Filtration Membranes (NF) that selectively separate ions and organic molecules. Additionally, there are other membrane types such as Electrodialysis Membranes and Gas Separation Membranes that serve specific filtration purposes in industries such as water treatment, food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and more. These membranes play a vital role in purifying and separating substances efficiently.</p></p>
