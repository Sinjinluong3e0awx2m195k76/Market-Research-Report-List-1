<p><strong>The LED Leadframe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Simplex Metal Lead Frame</li><li>Resin Molding Lead Frame</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LED Leadframe Market consists of two main types: Simplex Metal Lead Frame and Resin Molding Lead Frame. Simplex Metal Lead Frame is made of a single metal material, providing higher thermal conductivity and better electrical performance. On the other hand, Resin Molding Lead Frame uses a combination of metal and resin materials, offering increased design flexibility and cost-effectiveness. Both types cater to different needs in the LED industry, with simplex metal lead frame being more traditional and resin molding lead frame offering newer technological advancements.</p></p>
