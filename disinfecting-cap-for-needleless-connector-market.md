<p><p>Disinfecting caps for needleless connectors come in two main types: single cap system and double cap system. Single cap system refers to a cap that covers the connector with one cap, providing a convenient and easy-to-use solution for disinfection. On the other hand, double cap system involves two separate caps - one for sealing the connector and another for disinfection, offering an added layer of protection against contamination. Both systems aim to prevent infections and ensure the safety of healthcare procedures involving needleless connectors.</p></p>
