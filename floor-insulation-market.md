<p><ul><li>Interior</li><li>Exterior</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Floor insulation is commonly used in both interior and exterior applications to improve energy efficiency and thermal comfort. Interior floor insulation is installed between the floor joists to prevent heat loss and reduce energy consumption. Exterior floor insulation is used beneath the floor surface to provide additional thermal resistance and protect against moisture damage. Both applications help regulate indoor temperatures, reduce heating and cooling costs, and create a more comfortable living environment. These solutions are essential in residential, commercial, and industrial buildings.</p></p>
